About Gain_Campaign >>

The Group Gain_Campaing the outdoor CrossFit brainchild of 3 Zillow employees during the quarantine of 2020. Forced to chase gains at home or in outdoor spaces in solitude. The three Cody M., Paul R., And Adama S. decided to begin documenting their workouts at home and outside and post them to Instagram to encourage each other to stay consistent and motivate others with fitness routines and ideas to target muscle groups without access to gyms and limited personal equipment. Introducing: Gain_Campaign the web app.

Gain_Campaign the Web app allows fitness seekers to keep track of their workouts and finess gains. It is core the Gain_campaign web app is just about building a healthy fitness community through a social platform and providing access to free workouts by fitness professionals.

No calorie counter or diet measures, and no judgment. Positive support, motivation, happy bodies, and a lifetime fitness community!

How to Use >>

Your Gain_campaign account is a virtual reminder of all your workouts, meals and fitness gains you've had over the years. and a platform to share that journey with others!
